Red Planet Expedition Itinerary

Duration: 10 Days

Days 1-2

Arrival, acclimatization, and introductory colony tour.

Day 3

Rover expedition to Gale Crater, exploring sedimentary formations.

Day 4

Visit to Valles Marineris with a guide sharing its history.

Day 5

Trek to the northern polar ice cap.

Day 6

Overnight under the Martian sky in a portable observatory.

Days 7-8

Excursion to Tharsis, exploring volcanoes like Arsia Mons.

Day 9

Free exploration day with a guide.

Day 10

Return journey to Earth.

Price: 60 000 Euro

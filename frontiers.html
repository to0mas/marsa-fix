Frontiers of Mars Itinerary

Duration: 14 Days

Days 1-3

Settle into the colony, introductory lectures, and colony tours.

Days 4-5

Exploration of Hellas Planitia, the deepest crater on Mars.

Days 6-7

Expedition through Noctis Labyrinthus' winding canyons.

Day 8

Journey to the southern polar ice cap.

Days 9-10

Adventure through the volcanic region, including Olympus Mons.

Day 11

Field mission in Meridiani Planum.

Days 12-13

Relaxation and documentation of natural wonders.

Day 14

Farewell and return to Earth.

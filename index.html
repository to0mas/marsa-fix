Mars Rover

This all-terrain vehicle is crafted for Martian conditions, enabling safe and in-depth surface exploration. Equipped with high-resolution cameras, an air filtration system, navigation technology, and a robotic arm, it’s ready for detailed analysis on Mars.

Mars suit

A suit designed for movement on Mars, protecting against extreme temperatures and radiation while ensuring a supply of oxygen. Equipment: Thermal regulation, air filtration, communication devices, helmet with a wide field of view.

Skibidi Mars

Skibidi-Mars is a modern spacecraft designed for an unforgettable and stylish journey to Mars. It combines the latest technology with a casual atmosphere, offering passengers not only comfort but also a space experience with a touch of adventure.

Upcoming Mars Expeditions

2026 - Mars Discovery

This introductory journey is perfect for first-time Mars explorers. Enjoy guided tours of Mars base camps, safe areas, and Mars’s basic terrain. The ideal choice for those looking to explore Mars with minimal risk.


2028 - Red Head Planet

This expedition offers a more immersive experience, focusing on Mars’s geology and landscape. You'll journey through unique Martian sites and gain hands-on experience with exploration equipment. Designed for those with a spirit for scientific discovery and moderate adventure.


2030 - Frontiers of Mars

A challenging journey into Mars’s rugged terrain and less-traveled areas, including Olympus Mons and the vast Valles Marineris canyon. Perfect for adventure-seekers ready to experience Mars’s wild frontiers.


Mars expeditions come with high risks, including the potential that travelers may not return safely. Each journey requires courage, resilience, and an openness to the unknown.
